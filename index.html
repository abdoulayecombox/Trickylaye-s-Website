Trickylaye

Producer, DJ, Singer

I am a Musician, Deejay and producer of Paris. Very passionated by electronic music I started playing drums, piano and guitar. My influences are diverse and range from Alain Souchon to Arman Van Helden without forgetting Bob Marley, RZA or Timbaland. The piece of Afro-Caribbean inspiration “Oui baby” marked the beginning of my musical journey in 2003. I launched Several project under the tags, Trickylaye, Mr Doo or Araftu Project. I am also booked as a DJ or live artist for high level private parties
